About Johnson Grammar School 
• What is Johnson Grammar School? Johnson Grammar School (JGS) is a co‑educational institution with campuses at LB Nagar and Kuntloor in 
Hyderabad. It provides quality education rooted in core values and ethics and has been treading the path of excellence since its inception in 
2009 1 .

• Which curriculum does JGS follow? JGS follows the ICSE curriculum. The school notes that the Council for the Indian School Certificate 
Examination (New Delhi) aims to provide high‑quality education and exciting learning opportunities . 
• How many teachers are there? JGS’s academic section mentions that the school has a well‑qualified and highly trained staff of over 600 
educators, bringing diverse talent and enthusiasm to every classroom . 
Mission, Vision & Values 
• Mission of the school: The mission of Johnson Grammar School is to prepare each student for holistic learning and development by providing 
opportunities in a stimulating, safe and supportive environment. The school challenges all students to realize their physical, intellectual, 
emotional and social potentials so they become proactive, responsible and globally minded citizens 6 . 
• Vision of the school: JGS envisions igniting in young minds a love of learning and continuous development by making learning an enjoyable 
experience. It aims to develop students’ capabilities from a global perspective by instilling self‑esteem and confidence, achieve excellence by 
motivating students to aim high, and pioneer “next practices” that promote an international outlook 7 . 
• Values and ethics: The school believes that education combines knowledge acquisition, opportunities and value systems. Holistic 
development is a mantra at JGS; inculcating values is the culmination of all experiences. The LB Nagar and Kuntloor campuses were established 
with a motto to provide quality education with an emphasis on core values and ethics 1 .

Admissions 
• How can I start the admission process? Begin by submitting an enquiry. You may call +91 9652260499 or +91 9391551065 or fill out the 
online enquiry form. A counselor will then contact you to guide you through the process . 
• What are the steps in the admissions process? 
• Enquiry: Contact the school or fill out the enquiry form . 
• Consultation: Meet or speak with a counselor to discuss the school’s educational approach and clarify any questions . 
• Campus visit: Parents are encouraged to visit the campus to experience its culture and facilities 10 . 
• Application form: After counseling, purchase an application form to proceed 11 . 
• Interaction/assessment: Students undergo age‑appropriate interactions or assessments (oral for kindergarten, written for higher classes) 12 . 
• Confirmation & fee payment: After completing the steps, parents receive an admission offer and secure enrollment by paying the relevant 
fees 13 .

Academics & Curriculum 
• What is the school’s educational philosophy? JGS’s core values and educational beliefs are governed by its mission to provide quality and 
affordable education and guide students to “aim high” and achieve success 32 . 
• Tell me about the curriculum. The curriculum is based on the ICSE board; it is designed to offer high‑quality education and create exciting 
learning opportunities, empowering students to excel in all fields . 
• What does ICSE education emphasise at JGS? It emphasises skill development, creativity and real‑world application. Students learn to 
explore, analyze, question and communicate, turning them into innovators and problem‑solvers 33 . The curriculum balances academics, 
co‑curricular activities and values‑based learning, encouraging students to connect classroom learning with real‑world experiences 34 . 
• Which skills are developed through the ICSE curriculum? Key skills include: 
• Critical thinking and application‑based learning: Students are encouraged to question, reason and apply concepts to real‑life situations 35 . 
• Communication and collaboration: Group discussions, debates and drama activities help students express themselves clearly and work 
effectively with others 36 . 
• Creativity and innovation: Art, music, literature and design are used to build imagination and originality 37 . 
• Digital literacy: Technology is integrated through computer applications, coding and interactive lessons to develop digital fluency 38 . 
• Problem‑solving and decision‑making: Students tackle case studies and challenges requiring logic and creativity 39 . 
• Leadership and teamwork: Sports, house activities and cultural programmes teach leadership, discipline and cooperation 40 . 
• Emotional intelligence and values‑based learning: Discussion and moral education help children learn empathy, kindness and responsibility 
41 . 
• How does JGS bring learning to life? Teachers focus on application‑driven teaching — theory is turned into experience through experiments 
in science, puzzles in mathematics and storytelling in language. Projects sharpen observation, creativity and critical thinking 42 . 
Beyond Academics 
• What does “Beyond Academics” mean at JGS? While academic excellence is essential, JGS integrates extracurricular and co‑curricular 
activities with academics to ensure all‑round development. This blend encourages learning beyond the classroom 43 . 
• What activities are available? Activities cultivate curiosity, ethics and values. Opportunities include leadership roles in the school cabinet, 
participation in the school choir, membership in the ECO Club and SPACE Club, and engagement in sports and games 44 .
